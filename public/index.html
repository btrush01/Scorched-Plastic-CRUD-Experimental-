Scorched Plastic

An Army Men Wargame

If you're like me, you grew up playing with little Plastic Army Men, but as the years passed, I found myself wanting something more sophisticated than throwing marbles at the opposing side. Still, it's Plastic Army Men; simplicity should be the name of the game. Yet, rulesets I found for organized tabletop play were all overly complex, so I made my own. This site will teach the absolute basics of the game, which I have named "Scorched Plastic". That means, we are playing mostly on a soldier vs soldier level, with few to no vehicles. However, because of the ease in obtaining the stereotypical Timmee figures upon which this game is based, building larger forces is more feasible than with typical expensive wargaming figures.

  • 2+ players
  • Army Men figures/accessories
  • 4 dice
  • Measuring tape
